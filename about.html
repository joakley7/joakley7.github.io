    About Me:

    My name is John Oakley! I am a senior at Kennesaw State University majoring in Information Technology with my concentration in Cyber Operations Security. I currently do school, work as an undergrad researcher, and at a CO-OP 5 days a week for IT Enterprise Systems and Analyst. I’ve built computers for some of my high school teachers, family, friends, and myself many times. I also like playing bass guitar. I haven’t performed in front of a crowd in quite some time though.
